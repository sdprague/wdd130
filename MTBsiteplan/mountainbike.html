Welcome to Rexburg Mountain Bike Club

Mountain biking is an exhilarating outdoor sport that combines the thrill of adventure with the beauty of nature. Whether you’re a beginner or a seasoned rider, there’s always a new trail to explore. On this website, you will find tips, information about the best trails, and advice on biking gear to help you get the most out of your mountain biking experience here in Rexburg.

About Mountain Biking

Mountain biking involves riding bicycles on rugged terrain, typically found on mountain trails, dirt roads, or through forests. Riders encounter different challenges such as steep climbs, descents, and obstacles like rocks, roots, and streams. It's a sport that combines skill, strength, and an appreciation for the natural world.

Benefits of Mountain Biking

  • Improves cardiovascular health
  • Builds strength and endurance
  • Enhances balance and coordination
  • Allows you to explore the great outdoors
  • Reduces stress and improves mental well-being
